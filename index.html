--->Blood cannot be manufactured in factories; it can only come from generous donors. To cater to this demand, Blood bank india is organising a blood donation with an unparalleled objective of solving the problem of blood shortage in India. According to WHO data, India faces a shortage of 3 million blood units. This shortage can easily be eliminated if only an additional 2% of India's youth donates blood. To make this possible, Blood bank india acts as a channel connecting voluntary blood donors with those who need blood. It is a youth-run organization and provides free help and specially works to target the poor and the needy.

Objective:

->To create awareness and motivate People for Blood Donation

->To collect blood units for future use in emergency cases.

->To bring awareness for blood donation by removing misconceptions related to blood transfusion.

->To help patients of dyslexia and other diseases related to blood.

->To provide student with a platform for providing their services to society.


Role of Volunteers:

->To Donate Blood and contribute to this Nobel Cause.
